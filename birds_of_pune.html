Birds of Pune ~ feathered friends around...


Pune, having the blessings of the nature, being surrounded by the western ghats hills, also has huge catchment areas for dams, ever-flowing rivers and arid grasslands resulting in a very rich and diverse wildlife, specially the birds. It is a popular destination for migrating birds too making Pune a special treat in winter time.


Few places around Pune that are rich in avian life :
1. ARAI hills in Kothrud.
2. kavadi Pat on Pune-Solapur Highway
3. Bhigwan (Kumbhargaoo) on Pune Solapur Highway
4. Veer Dam near Shirwal on NH4
5. Bhuleshwar Temple, Saswad grasslands
6. Sinhgadh Valley
7. Tamhini Ghat

Explore more Birding Hotspots on eBird too.

Don't forget to check out this facebook group "Birds of Pune" that connects and shares the love for birds among people in pune.
