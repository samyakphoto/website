Kabini (Nagarhole National Forest), Karnataka.


The Jungle Lodges at Kabini is strategically situated on the banks of Kabini River.
Once it used to be the hunting lodge of Maharaja of Mysore. Its huge campus with neatly maintained landscapes and superb food and hospitality will make your stay worthwhile there.
They offer jeep safaris into deep Nagarhole National forest and also the boat rides which take you through the kabini river along the bankside for birding.
