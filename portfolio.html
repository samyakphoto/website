7. A Huge Himalayan Ibex (Capra ibex sibirica) treading in snow during winter in Himalaya.
Location : Kibber Wildlife Sanctuary, Himachal Pradesh, India.

Location : Kibber Wildlife Sanctuary, Himachal Pradesh, India.



9. The snow leopard (Panthera uncia) looking back at the camera.
Location : Kibber Wildlife Sanctuary, Himachal Pradesh, India.



10. The bonnet macaque (Macaca radiata) grooming each other as part of their social activity.
Location : Varhanda Ghat, Pune, Maharashtra, India.



11. Greater Flamingo (Phoenicopterus roseus) walks across in shallow waters of backwaters.
Location : Bhigwan Bird Sanctuary, Maharashtra, India.



12. The Himalayan vulture (Gyps himalayensis) fight with each other over the rights of feeding on a dead cattle.
Location : Shimla, Himachal Pradesh, India.

Location : The Hoollongapar Gibbon Sanctuary, Assam, India.
