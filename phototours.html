Photography Mentor



Samyak Kaninde

Samyak is an experienced and award-winning Wildlife Photographer. He is a keen naturalist and loves to spend time observing the wild. He has special love for High Altitude mountains along with north eastern forests for the unique flora and fauna that inhabit these inaccessible places.

He has an experience of more than a decade in nature and wildlife photography. His images not just represent the aesthetic beauty of nature but also capture the stress and problems, the natural world habitat is facing. His images have helped to catch and fine the poachers, highlight conservation issues and awareness campaigns. He strongly believes that 'photography is an important tool for not just bringing out the beauty of nature but making a difference to save what is remaining around us'.

His photo stories and nature writings are published in various magazines, newspapers, coffee table books & nature education materials.
