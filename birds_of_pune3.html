The bar-headed goose (Anser indicus) pair
Kumbhargao, Bhigwan, Pune.

The common teal (Anas crecca) male.
Kavadi Pat, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F8, 1/640s, ISO 200, -1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The short-toed snake eagle (Circaetus gallicus) with snake in its talons.
Dive ghat, Saswad, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/4000s, ISO 200, -1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The red-necked falcon (Falco chicquera) with the kill - One lazy afternoon, after hearing all the frantic calls of birds, I looked around to find this little beauty tearing apart the feathers of its kill on my apartment balcony's poll.
Hadapsar, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F8, 1/100s, ISO 250, +2/3 Ev, Tripod.


Pallid Harrier (Circus macrourus) juvenile landing with the locust/grasshopper in its talons.
Dive Ghat, Saswad, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/3200s, ISO 320, -2/3 Ev, Tripod.


The black-shouldered kite (Elanus axillaris) preying on garden lizard.
Dive Ghat, Saswad, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/800s, ISO 250, -1/3 Ev.


The Eurasian hobby (Falco subbuteo) - another passage migrant to Pune. This was one of the fastest of dash I was seeing in air, it was hard to track it and get in focus.
Dive ghat, Saswad, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/2000s, ISO 250, +1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The common kestrel (Falco tinnunculus)

Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/640s, ISO 250, Tripod.


The southern grey shrike (Lanius meridionalis)

The wood sandpiper (Tringa glareola)
