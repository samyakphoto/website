Canon 550D, Canon 10-22mm
30s, F16, ISO 100, Tripod.


12. The mother elephant with its calf coming for water and stroll alongside Kabini River.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/640s, F5.6, ISO 200, Handheld from boat.


13. The spotted deers are found everywhere and they run behind each other whenever they feel scared or alarmed.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/60s, F5.6, ISO 400, -1/3 Ev, Handheld.


14. Spot Billed Duck (Anas poecilorhyncha) in flight passing over our boat.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/500s, F6.3, ISO 200, Handheld.


15. Little Egret (Egretta garzetta) on a perch in Kabini River.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/1600s, F5.6, ISO 200, Handheld.


16. Oriental Darter (Anhinga melanogaster) aka "snakebird" called due to its twisted neck appearance which resembles a snake when they swim with body submerged in water.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/800s, F5.6, ISO 200, Handheld.


17. Elephant mother and its new born calf. The calf was hardly able to walk.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/50s, F5.6, ISO 200, Handheld.


18. The cattle egret marching onwards on a perch in Kabini River. The perch is also used to polish/clean the beak by rubbing beak against the rough woodlock.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/2500s, F5.6, ISO 400, -1/3 Ev, Handheld.


19. Osprey (Pandion haliaetus) is about to take off from its perch.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/640s, F5.6, ISO 400, Handheld.


20. Large Cormorant (Phalacrocorax carbo) nearby its nest on an old woodlock in Kabini River.

Canon 400D, Canon 300mm with 1.4 TC
1/2500s, F5.6, ISO 400, Handheld.
