Sarpass Trek - Himalayan Landscapes


These are the pictures I have taken in my trip to Himachal Pradesh for a sarpass trek with YHAI.
The base camp was located at 6800ft near Kullu in a village called as Kasol.
The sarpass was at 13,800 ft to be done in 7 days trek. The trek was completed successfully. Below images would try to showcase the beauty that might himalaya beholds !

22. From nagaru camp, we have to start trek at 4 in the morning to avoid the melting snow due to sun. Climbing this at 4am was quite a task.
Canon 5D MII, Canon 24-105mm.


23. The dog named "lalit" followed us throughout the trek and made it very easily on the snow too.
Canon 5D MII, Canon 24-105mm.


24. The sherpa making the way and the dog following him.

25. Someones backpack just rolled over and added the punch to this ice-scape.

26. Snow avalanches are the most dangerous of stretches and you need to pass them fast. Once such avalanche pass.
Canon 5D MII, Canon 24-105mm.


27. The water stream keeps running under the ice and sometimes form these shapes where the ice melts fast. Do not miss the person in left corner sliding down and a group far behind him.

28. The icy valleys.
