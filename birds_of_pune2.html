The garganey (Anas querquedula)
Kumbhargao, Bhigwan, Pune.
Canon 7D Mark II, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/1250s, ISO 250, Tripod.


The black-tailed godwit (Limosa limosa) - a Winter migrant in its breeding colors just before they leave for their breeding grounds.
Kumbhargao, Bhigwan, Pune.
Canon 7D Mark II, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/1250s, ISO 250, Tripod.


The Indian pond heron(Ardeola grayii) in its breeding colors.
Kumbhargao, Bhigwan, Pune.
Canon 7D Mark II, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/1250s, ISO 250, Tripod.


The greater flamingo (Phoenicopterus roseus) - Normally, they feed on the microscopic algae under water but here it is seen swallowing something caught from under water. They are seen in thousands around Bhigwan in each winter season. It is a treat to observe and photograph them.
Kumbhargao, Bhigwan, Pune.
Canon 7D Mark II, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/3200s, ISO 250, -1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The black-headed ibis (Threskiornis melanocephalus)
Kumbhargao, Bhigwan, Pune.
Canon 7D Mark II, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/2500, ISO 250, Tripod.


The white-throated kingfisher (Halcyon smyrnensis)
kavadi Pat, Pune.

The white-bellied minivet (Pericrocotus erythropygius)
Bhuleshwar, Saswad, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/500s, ISO 320, +1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The black drongo (Dicrurus macrocercus) with wandering glider dragonfly as its meal.
Dive Ghat, Saswad, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F8, 1/800s, ISO 250, -1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The grey heron (Ardea cinerea) breaking into a dance pose.
kavadi Pat, Pune.
Canon 7D, canon 500mm, 1.4 TC, F5.6, 1/320s, ISO 200, -1/3 Ev, Tripod.


The ruddy shelduck (Tadorna ferruginea) doing a stretching exercise.
kavadi Pat, Pune.
